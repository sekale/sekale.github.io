Minimalist Design Principles

A lot of my opinions about this content is somewhat derivative of this BB article I was attending a product design class, and someone pointed out that bland seems to be the default choice for colors and design. Bland = Do one thing well + under-advertise on your product Minimalistic design with neutral colors: Gone are the days where flashy colors on an HTML5 (with some cool JS animations) was the major seller. Neutral pastel colors now take precedence and noisy UIs are replaced with relevant contextualized blocks. In product they come off as the under-confident backbencher who challenges the status quo and makes big wins. I think the trend is mostly a net-positive but it exemplifies a growing trend of marketing a product as doing one thing but really well. (More...)
